## 1. Flat "yeah" after pitch

**Stage:** GREETING | **Transcripts:** 3

| Speaker | Message |
|---------|---------|
| Agent | Hi, this is Caesar from Simple.Biz. We're local website designers here in Las Vegas. |
| Agent | We're just wondering if you're interested in building or updating your website, since we're super affordable. |
| Customer | Yeah. |

**AI Tip:** Re-engage Now (ENGAGEMENT)
**Why:** Customer gave a dry, one-word response ("Yeah") after the pitch — unclear if they're interested or just acknowledging.
**Script:** "Do you currently have a website for your business, or is this something you've been thinking about setting up?"
